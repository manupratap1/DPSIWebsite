Auditorium:

The school boasts of an auditorium with a seating capacity of 500. It is equipped with state of the art sound and light facilities and is used for major school functions, theatre and dance presentations and for guest lectures.

The School Library:

Over 20,000 books, journals and magazines are available in the school library. International publications and CDs are available as resources for reference work.

The library is divided into five sections.

Teacher's Resource.
Primary Level Library
Senior & Middle Level Library
Audio visual Library
Smart Class (For e Classroom)

Class rooms upto standard VIII are also equipped with class libraries that are regularly updated.

Language Lab:

A well equipped language lab is used to supplement and support the teaching of English, French, German, Spanish and other foreign languages.

Maths Lab:

A Math Lab is a space designed for students to learn mathematics by performing activities and getting hands on experience. It provides a wide variety of materials to play with and learn the concepts. Math teaching and learning for primary school level (i.e for class Nur to V) can entirely be done with activities and experiments by students. Math Lab also encourages group learning and co-operative learning among children. It encourages the 'do and discover' method of learning, removes the fear of Math and thus complements classroom learning.

Sick Bay/ Infirmary :

There is a doctor and trained nurse available on the school premises during the working hours of the school . The medical room caters to routine medical problems. All children undergo an annual medical check up and a health file is maintained for each student. In the event of an emergency, children are sent to a nearby hospital, which is well equipped with competent staff and facilities.

Canteen & Cafeteria:

The school canteen serves fresh and nutritious snacks, health drinks and juices. Hot lunch is available as an optional facility on extra monthly payment.
